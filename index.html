About Nzhelele FM

Nzhelele FM is a vibrant community radio station based in the Thohoyandou/Louis Trichardt region of Limpopo. Broadcasting on 103.0 FM, it blends music, talk, news, and cultural content to reflect the heartbeat of its community. With over 21,000 followers on Facebook, it’s the voice of the people—connecting, informing, and celebrating local life.
